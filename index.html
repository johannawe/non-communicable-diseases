About this project

This was an experimental students project created at HfG Schwäbisch Gmünd.

The initial objective of the visualisation was to develop an interaction and visual system that would work without the use of text or icons, displaying multiple levels of information.

Navigation Patterns

Basis for the interaction patterns were that the graphic should be navigatable without the use of additional buttons.
